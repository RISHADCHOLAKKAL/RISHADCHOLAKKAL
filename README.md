# 💫 About Me:
I am currently a student at St. Mary’s College, Sulthan Bathery, where I am building a strong academic foundation while actively exploring my passion for technology and creativity. I have a growing interest in the fields of web designing and graphic designing, where I enjoy combining logic with visual creativity to build engaging digital experiences.

I am particularly interested in creating modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I enjoy experimenting with layouts, colors, and typography to design interfaces that are both visually appealing and functional. Alongside web design, I also explore graphic design, where I work on creating visuals that communicate ideas clearly and creatively.

As a learner, I am continuously improving my skills by working on personal projects and exploring new tools and technologies. I am especially focused on developing clean design practices, improving user experience, and understanding how design and development work together.

I am always eager to learn, experiment, and grow as a designer and developer, with the goal of building impactful digital solutions in the future.
